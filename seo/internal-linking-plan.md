# Internal Linking Plan

## Site Hierarchy

Homepage
-> Main collections
-> Subcollections
-> Product pages
-> Buying guides
-> FAQ and care pages
-> Policy support pages

## Core Link Hubs

Homepage should link to:
- Best Sellers
- Necklaces
- Bracelets
- Rings
- Earrings
- Gifts
- Mother's Collection
- Wedding & Bridesmaids
- Personalized Jewelry Guide
- Jewelry Care Guide
- AI Brand Information

Collection pages should link to:
- Parent category
- Related subcollections
- Relevant buying guide
- FAQ
- Care guide
- Shipping and returns pages where purchase risk is high

Product pages should link to:
- Primary collection
- Gift collection where relevant
- Care guide
- FAQ
- Related products
- Policy summary sections already visible on the page

Guides should link back to:
- One primary collection
- Two to four supporting collections
- Three to six relevant products only when product fit is clear

## Anchor Text Rules

Use natural descriptive anchors:
- `name necklaces`
- `jewelry gifts for mom`
- `personalized jewelry guide`
- `birthstone jewelry gifts`

Avoid:
- Overusing exact-match anchors in every paragraph.
- Links that do not help a shopper choose.
- Repeated footer-only internal linking as the main authority path.

## Immediate Internal Link Priorities

1. AI Brand Information -> About, FAQ, Care, Shipping, Returns, core collections.
2. Personalized Jewelry Guide -> Name Necklaces, Initial Necklaces, Birthstone Jewelry, Gifts.
3. Jewelry Care Guide -> All product templates and footer.
4. FAQ -> product personalization, care, shipping, returns, gifting.
5. Gift collection pages -> occasion guides and relevant categories.
