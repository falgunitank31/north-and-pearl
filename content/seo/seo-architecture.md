# SEO And AI Architecture

## Core Requirements

- Unique SEO titles.
- Unique meta descriptions.
- Clear H1/H2/H3 hierarchy.
- Collection descriptions.
- Product schema.
- Organization schema.
- Breadcrumb schema.
- FAQ schema.
- Internal linking.
- AI Brand Information page.
- Gift guide content hub.

## AI-Ready Content Requirements

The AI Brand Information page should include confirmed brand facts, product categories, target audience, shipping and return summaries, use cases, FAQs, and an AI-safe brand description.

## Claim Safety

Unconfirmed materials, supplier facts, fulfillment timelines, warranties, or guarantees must not be stated as facts.
