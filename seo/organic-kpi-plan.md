# Organic KPI Plan

Do not report performance until analytics sources are verified.

## Required Sources

- Shopify Analytics.
- Google Search Console.
- GA4.
- Bing Webmaster Tools.
- Pinterest analytics.
- Google Merchant Center free listings.

## KPI Framework

Visibility:
- Indexed pages.
- Search impressions.
- Non-brand clicks.
- Brand clicks.
- Query growth by collection topic.
- Image search impressions.
- Free Shopping impressions.

Engagement:
- Organic sessions.
- Organic landing page engagement.
- Product views from organic.
- Collection views from organic.
- Email signups from organic.

Conversion:
- Add-to-cart rate by organic landing page.
- Checkout starts from organic.
- Purchases from organic.
- Organic revenue.
- Assisted revenue.
- New customer revenue from organic.

AI Discovery:
- AI referral sessions where visible.
- Referral traffic from Perplexity, ChatGPT, Gemini, Bing/Copilot surfaces when analytics exposes them.
- Branded query growth after AI-ready content launches.

## Measurement Rules

- Baselines must be collected after tracking is confirmed.
- Do not invent search volume or traffic estimates.
- Use month-over-month and 90-day trend reporting once enough data exists.
