# Product Publication Checklist

Before a product is live:

- Product has accurate customer-facing title, description, price, and availability.
- Product has usable images, useful alt text, and no supplier branding/watermark overlays.
- Product has correct collection assignments and tags.
- Product state is clear: Add to cart, Select options, Personalize, Notify me, or Sold out.
- Personalized products have relevant required fields and cart line-item properties.
- No internal notes, sourcing notes, supplier warnings, draft copy, or unverified claims are visible.
- Compare-at pricing is valid and not artificial.
- Materials, dimensions, plating, stones, chain length, closure, and production/shipping facts are included only when verified.
- Search/Google/merchant fields do not invent identifiers, claims, or certifications.
- Live product page and collection card pass QA.
