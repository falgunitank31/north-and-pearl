## Summary

Improved empty customer-facing collections so shoppers do not land on thin dead-end pages.

- Pages rendered branded North & Pearl copy.
- Pages included collection links back into live product discovery paths.

## Business Impact

- Reduces dead-end browsing.

- Gives search engines clearer context without inventing product availability.
