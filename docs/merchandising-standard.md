# Merchandising Standard

The store should lead with product discovery and shopping clarity.

## Homepage

- Real product/lifestyle imagery before abstract brand explanation.
- Best Sellers or Featured Collection near the top.
- New Arrivals or second curated product grid.
- Image-led category and gift-intent cards.
- One concise brand story below core merchandising.

## Collections

- Products should appear early.
- Long SEO copy belongs below the grid or inside a disclosure.
- Do not expose empty collections in navigation or homepage merchandising.
- Sort and feature products by customer intent, visual quality, availability, and category fit.

## Product Cards

- Consistent image ratio, clear title, clear price, valid compare-at price only.
- No excessive badges, fake scarcity, or misleading quick-add behavior.
- Personalized items should send shoppers to the product page when required details are needed.
