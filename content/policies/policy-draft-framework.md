# Policy Draft Framework

Policy language must be reviewed by the owner before publishing.

## Shipping Policy

- State production time separately from transit time.
- Confirm free U.S. shipping conditions before publishing.
- Clarify carrier, tracking, lost package, incorrect address, and delivery exception handling.
- Do not promise timelines until supplier and fulfillment are confirmed.

## Returns & Exchanges

- Personalized and custom products are generally final sale unless defective, damaged, or incorrect.
- Standard items may use a 14-30 day return window after owner approval.
- Customers must contact support before sending returns.
- Returned standard items should be unused and in original packaging.

## Refund Policy

- Refunds should be issued to the original payment method after inspection and approval.
- Custom order issues should be handled through replacement, correction, or refund depending on the case.

## Custom Product Policy

- Customers are responsible for entering personalization accurately.
- North & Pearl should not produce offensive, infringing, or prohibited personalization.
- Character limits, symbols, accents, and preview limitations must be stated on product pages.

## Privacy And Terms

- Use Shopify-generated legal templates as a starting point inside admin, then customize with owner/legal review.
- Do not add tracking scripts until privacy review and consent requirements are addressed.
