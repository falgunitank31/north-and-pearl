# Product SEO Standards

## Purpose

Use this standard for North & Pearl product creation, product cleanup, and organic Shopping readiness. It is claim-safe by default: unverified material, stone, finish, allergy, durability, origin, handmade, sustainability, warranty, and fulfillment claims stay UNKNOWN until supplier documentation and owner approval are available.

## Required Product Fields

- Product title: unique, descriptive, customer-facing, and not differentiated only by random suffix.
- Handle: readable, stable, and aligned to the product title.
- SEO title: 50-70 characters where possible; include product type and brand.
- Meta description: 120-160 characters where possible; include buyer intent, gift use, or personalization use without unsupported claims.
- Product type/category: use consistent product taxonomy such as Necklace, Bracelet, Ring, Earrings, Jewelry Set, Gift Card, or Gift Box.
- Tags: use stable collection assignment tags; avoid messy one-off variants unless needed for merchandising.
- Product description: include buyer, occasion, personalization, visual/style detail, care, and claim boundary.
- Product media alt text: describe the visible product and product type without invented material.
- Variant options: reflect real customer choices only.
- Materials field: use `Pending confirmation` or product-specific verified facts only.

## Product Description Structure

1. Opening paragraph: what the product is, who it is for, and why it fits a meaningful gift moment.
2. `Why it makes a meaningful gift`: recipient and occasion fit.
3. `Details`: personalization options, style notes, and confirmed options.
4. `Materials`: only verified product-specific facts, or a clear pending-confirmation note.
5. `Care`: general care guidance unless material-specific care is confirmed.
6. `Before you order`: spelling/personalization review, production/shipping caveat, and final-sale caveat for custom pieces where policy confirms it.

## Safe Language

Safe when true for the product:

- personalized
- gift-ready
- birthstone-inspired
- name, initial, date, coordinate, or word personalization
- designed for birthdays, anniversaries, mothers, bridesmaids, couples, weddings, or milestones
- material details pending confirmation
- review product-specific details before checkout

Avoid unless verified:

- hypoallergenic
- waterproof
- tarnish-free
- nickel-free
- lead-free
- cadmium-free
- sterling silver
- gold vermeil
- solid gold
- ethically sourced
- handmade
- sustainable
- real gemstone
- guaranteed delivery date
- best seller, top rated, or customer favorite unless supported by actual data

## SEO Title Patterns

- `{Product Name} | Personalized Jewelry Gift | North & Pearl`
- `{Product Name} | Meaningful {Recipient/Occasion} Gift | North & Pearl`
- `{Product Type} for {Occasion/Recipient} | North & Pearl`

Do not stack too many modifiers. Use the most specific confirmed intent.

## Meta Description Patterns

- `Personalize {product type} for {recipient/occasion}. A meaningful jewelry gift with product-specific details and care notes to review before checkout.`
- `Shop {product name}, designed for {occasion/recipient}. Review personalization, material notes, and shipping details before ordering.`

## Image SEO Standards

- Primary image alt: `North & Pearl {Product Name} {product type} product image 1`.
- Detail image alt: describe visible angle or detail, e.g. `North & Pearl {Product Name} clasp detail`.
- Lifestyle image alt: describe use case, e.g. `{Product Name} styled as a birthday jewelry gift`.
- Do not include material, stone, waterproof, hypoallergenic, or durability claims unless verified.

## Duplicate Product Governance

Before publishing a product, check whether another live product has the same:

- product type
- visual shape
- recipient/occasion intent
- primary keyword
- title stem
- image set

If two products overlap, either differentiate the title/description/collection intent clearly or keep one unpublished until merchandising has a reason for both.

## Schema Expectations

Shopify Product structured data should expose:

- name
- image
- description
- brand
- category
- offer price
- price currency
- availability
- URL

Do not add AggregateRating or Review schema unless verified reviews exist and the review app/data source is compliant.

## Organic Shopping Readiness

Before submitting products to any organic Shopping surface, confirm:

- product title does not include unsupported material claims
- product image is clear and product-inspectable
- price and availability are accurate
- shipping and returns policies are complete
- variant options match real purchasable options
- Google product category is appropriate
- product identifiers are handled correctly based on product type and supplier data
- prohibited or restricted claims are absent
