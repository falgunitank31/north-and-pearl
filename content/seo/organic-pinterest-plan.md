# Organic Pinterest Plan

Date: 2026-07-20

Pinterest role: organic visual discovery for gift intent, jewelry inspiration, collection browsing, and guide traffic. This plan does not include paid Pinterest campaigns.

## Pin Types

Product Pins:
- Destination: product URL.
- Use exact product images only.
- Title format: `[Product type] Gift Idea | North & Pearl`
- Example: `Heart Pendant Necklace Gift Idea | North & Pearl`

Collection Pins:
- Destination: collection URL.
- Use a collage or accurate product grouping from that collection.
- Title format: `[Occasion/recipient] Jewelry Gifts`
- Example: `Jewelry Gifts for Mom`

Guide Pins:
- Destination: blog or guide URL.
- Use clean editorial graphics or product images that match the guide.
- Title format: `How to [Choose/Style/Care] [Topic]`
- Example: `How to Choose a Name Necklace`

## 30-Day Posting Cadence

- 3 product pins per week.
- 2 collection pins per week.
- 1 guide/educational pin per week.
- Refresh seasonal boards monthly.

## Guardrails

- Do not use misleading renders or images that change product size, shape, stone, color, or finish.
- Do not claim waterproof, tarnish-free, hypoallergenic, nickel-free, sterling silver, gold vermeil, solid gold, handmade, sustainable, or ethical sourcing unless verified.
- Use exact product imagery for product pins.
- Keep descriptions helpful and purchase-safe.
