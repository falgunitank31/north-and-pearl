# Product Claim Policy

North & Pearl must use claim-safe language until supplier documentation and owner approval confirm product facts.

## Claims Requiring Evidence

- Sterling silver, solid gold, gold vermeil, gold plated, 18K, stainless steel
- Hypoallergenic, nickel-free, lead-free, cadmium-free
- Waterproof, tarnish-free, tarnish-resistant
- Handmade, ethically sourced, sustainable
- Warranty, guaranteed delivery timing, easy returns
- Verified reviews, ratings, customer counts, press mentions

## Safe Default

When facts are missing, use neutral customer-facing language such as "gold-tone finish", "color-accented detail", "giftable design", or "materials and dimensions vary by product and should be reviewed before purchase" only where accurate.

Do not show internal phrases like "supplier claim", "to be verified", "details to confirm", or "sample required" on the storefront.
