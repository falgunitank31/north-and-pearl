# Policy Draft Plan

## Policies To Draft

- Shipping Policy
- Returns & Exchanges
- Refund Policy
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Custom Product Policy

## Rules

- Draft original policy language only.
- Do not copy competitor policies.
- Personalized/custom products should generally be non-returnable unless defective, damaged, or incorrect.
- Standard products may have a limited return window after owner review.
- Customer should contact support before returns.
- Production and shipping timelines must be confirmed before publication.

## Status

Draft planning only. Final policies require owner review and legal review if needed.
