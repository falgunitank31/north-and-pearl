# Organic Pinterest Plan

Date: 2026-07-20

Scope: organic Pinterest only. No paid pins, paid media, influencer spend, retargeting, or unsupported product claims.

## Goals

- Create commercial organic discovery paths into priority collections and gift guides.
- Use Pinterest as an evergreen visual search channel for gift, occasion, and style intent.
- Support the clean active catalog without making supplier-dependent material, stone, performance, shipping, or review claims.

## Claim-Safe Pin Language

Use:

- jewelry gift idea
- meaningful jewelry gift
- birthstone-inspired
- initial necklace idea
- heart necklace gift
- bracelet gift idea
- bridesmaid jewelry gift idea
- jewelry gifts for mom
- anniversary jewelry gift idea
- everyday jewelry style

Avoid unless verified:

- waterproof
- tarnish-free
- hypoallergenic
- nickel-free
- sterling silver
- gold vermeil
- solid gold
- real gemstone
- handmade
- sustainable
- guaranteed delivery
- best seller, top rated, or customer favorite without performance proof

## Weekly Organic Workflow

- Monday: publish 3 collection pins.
- Tuesday: publish 2 guide pins.
- Wednesday: publish 3 product-type pins.
- Thursday: publish 2 occasion pins.
- Friday: publish 2 care/style/support pins.
- Weekly review: record saves, outbound clicks, top boards, and top destinations. Do not invent results before data exists.

## Creative Standards

- Use vertical assets where possible.
- Show the product clearly.
- Use simple text overlays such as `Jewelry Gifts for Mom` or `Name Necklace Ideas`.
- Avoid overlays with material or shipping claims.
- Keep brand name visible but secondary to the search intent.
- Use one destination per pin.

## Measurement

Track only organic metrics:

- impressions
- saves
- outbound clicks
- top boards
- top pins
- destination URL clicks
- assisted blog/collection visits in analytics when available
