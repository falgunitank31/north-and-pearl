# Design System

## Direction

Luxury minimal ecommerce with warm, gift-focused emotional cues.

## Color Direction

- Ivory background
- Matte black text
- Champagne gold accents
- Warm taupe secondary sections
- Soft white product/gallery surfaces

## Typography Direction

- Elegant serif headings
- Clean sans-serif body copy
- Generous but mobile-friendly spacing

## Component Direction

- Full-width hero sections
- Clean navigation
- Trust bars
- Category tiles
- Product cards
- FAQ accordions
- Email capture blocks
- Mobile sticky calls to action

## Claim Safety

Material claims such as sterling silver, gold vermeil, hypoallergenic, waterproof, tarnish-free, nickel-free, or 18K plating must not be used until confirmed and approved.
