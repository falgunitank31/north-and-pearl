# North & Pearl Ecommerce Quality Standard

Every storefront change must be reviewed as a cross-functional ecommerce change, not a single-file edit.

## Required Review Lanes

- Kuhn: jewelry UX, visual design, mobile layout, product-card and collection/product-page presentation.
- Gauss: merchandising, catalog data, collection assignment, product-state accuracy, product-image readiness.
- Faraday: SEO, AEO, GEO, internal links, metadata, schema, content intent, AI-readable facts.
- Tesla: Shopify theme code, Liquid, CSS, JavaScript, performance, cart/search/filter behavior, Theme Check.
- Rawls: analytics implications, KPI measurement, reporting impact.
- Lovelace: shipping, returns, support, fulfillment, policy-operational consistency.
- Curie: trust, claim verification, customer objections, material/supplier evidence, FAQ confidence.

## Release Gate

A task is complete only when applicable UX, catalog, SEO, trust, technical, and QA checks pass and the final report includes inspected areas, problems found, changes made, files/settings changed, tests, risks, assumptions, missing information, and live storefront validation.

## Never Publish

- Unsupported product or material claims
- Fake reviews, ratings, discounts, scarcity, press, customer counts, or UGC
- Internal supplier notes or draft instructions
- Empty collection links
- Contradictory product states
- Incomplete personalization workflows
- Placeholder customer copy
