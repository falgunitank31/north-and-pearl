# Brand Strategy

## Positioning

North & Pearl is a premium personalized jewelry and gifting brand focused on timeless, meaningful pieces for life's important moments.

## Promise

Crafted for life's meaningful moments.

## Brand Feel

- Premium
- Elegant
- Warm
- Trustworthy
- Gift-focused
- Minimal
- Modern luxury

## Brand Guardrails

- Use original copy and visual direction.
- Do not copy competitor language, designs, imagery, product names, or policies.
- Avoid exaggerated claims.
- Do not make product/material claims until supplier facts are confirmed.
