# Internal Linking Execution Checklist

Date: 2026-07-20

Scope: organic internal linking for homepage, collections, guides, and products. This is an implementation checklist, not a published-change log.

## Rules

- Use natural anchors that describe the destination.
- Put commercial links early enough to be useful.
- Do not overlink every paragraph.
- Do not link to empty, draft, or thin pages without orchestration.
- Do not use unsupported claims in anchor text.
- Keep links useful on mobile.

Safe commercial anchors:

- personalized jewelry gifts
- jewelry gifts for her
- name necklaces

- birthstone-inspired jewelry
- jewelry gifts for mom
- bridesmaid jewelry gifts
- anniversary jewelry gifts
- bracelet gift ideas
- necklace gift ideas
- jewelry care guide

Avoid anchors unless verified:

- waterproof jewelry
- tarnish-free jewelry
- hypoallergenic earrings
- sterling silver jewelry
- gold vermeil jewelry
- real gemstone jewelry
- guaranteed delivery gifts
- best-selling jewelry, unless sales data confirms

## QA Checklist

- Link opens to intended URL.
- Destination page is live or approved for implementation.
- Anchor describes destination accurately.
- No unsupported claim in anchor.
- Link placement helps the shopper continue.
- Mobile layout remains readable.
- No duplicate links crowded in the same paragraph.
- New guide links are added back to related collections.
