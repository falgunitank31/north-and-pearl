# North & Pearl Design Sprint

## Scope

This sprint applies a premium North & Pearl visual foundation to the Dawn draft theme and adds editable page templates for launch-critical content.

## Design Direction

- Ivory, warm taupe, matte black, and champagne gold accents.
- Serif headings with clean sans-serif body text.
- Mobile-first spacing, square product imagery, restrained cards, and clear CTAs.
- Original copy only. No competitor copy, images, layouts, or material claims.

## Implemented Theme Areas

- Global styling layer in `assets/north-pearl.css`.
- Homepage care/value and UGC placeholder sections.
- Footer newsletter copy refinement.
- About, FAQ, AI Brand Information, Jewelry Care, Personalized Jewelry Guide, Shipping Policy, and Returns & Exchanges templates.

## Manual Review Needed

- Replace placeholder imagery with original product and lifestyle photography.
- Confirm product materials before adding claims.
- Finalize policy language with owner and legal review.
- Assign templates to Shopify pages in the admin after pages are created.
