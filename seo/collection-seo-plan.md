## Collection Copy Formula

Top intro, 80-140 words:
- Say what the collection includes.
- Identify who it is for.
- Mention gifting occasions.
- Avoid unsupported materials and guarantees.
- Link to one related guide or collection.

Bottom SEO block, 180-280 words:
- Explain how to choose.
- Mention related categories.
- Answer 2-3 common questions.
- Add internal links to policies, care, personalization, and related collections.

## Collection Metadata Formula

SEO title:
- `Primary Topic | North & Pearl`
- Keep near 45-60 characters when possible.

Meta description:
- State product type, audience, and gift angle.
- Keep near 140-160 characters.
- Avoid unverified claims.

Example:
- Title: `Name Necklaces | North & Pearl`
- Description: `Shop elegant name necklaces designed for meaningful gifts, birthdays, anniversaries, mothers, bridesmaids, and everyday personalization.`
