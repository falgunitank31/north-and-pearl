# North & Pearl Logo System

## Concept

The logo combines an elegant serif wordmark with a small pearl-and-north-star mark. The mark represents direction, memory, and a meaningful keepsake.

## Assets

- `assets/north-pearl-logo.svg`: primary dark wordmark for light backgrounds.
- `assets/north-pearl-logo-light.svg`: light wordmark for dark backgrounds.
- `assets/north-pearl-mark.svg`: icon mark for favicon, packaging seals, social avatars, and small placements.

## Usage

- Use the primary wordmark in the Shopify header.
- Use the light wordmark on matte black packaging, dark hero panels, and dark footer placements.
- Use the mark when the full wordmark would be too small.

## Colors

- Matte black: `#171411`
- Ivory: `#fffaf3`
- Champagne gold: `#c6a15b`

## Restrictions

- Do not stretch, rotate, outline, add shadows, or place on low-contrast backgrounds.
- Do not replace the serif wordmark with a script font.
- Do not combine the logo with unconfirmed material claims.
