# Measurement Plan

## Goals

- Measure ecommerce conversion rate.
- Track product discovery paths.
- Track personalization intent.
- Track cart and checkout funnel behavior.
- Track email signup performance.

## Tools To Evaluate

- Shopify analytics
- GA4
- Microsoft Clarity
- Shopify Search & Discovery reporting

## Consent And Privacy

No analytics implementation should occur until tracking, consent, and privacy implications are reviewed.
