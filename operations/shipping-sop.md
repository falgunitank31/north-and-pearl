# Shipping SOP

## Status

Draft framework only.

## Direction

Shipping promises must be based on confirmed production, fulfillment, and carrier timelines.

## Dependency

Supplier production timelines and Shopify shipping setup must be confirmed before launch.

## Merchant Center Shipping Policy

Google Merchant Center requires account-level shipping information before products can fully show on Google surfaces.

For launch, North & Pearl is using one conservative account-level shipping policy for all U.S. products until fulfillment data is verified. Do not publish faster shipping promises until supplier production, inventory position, fulfillment handoff, and carrier performance are confirmed.

Configured in Google Merchant Center on 2026-07-22:

- Destination: United States
- Products: All products.
- Shipping cost: Free / $0.00.
- Handling time: 3-7 business days.
- Transit time: 5-10 business days.
- Displayed delivery estimate: 8-17 business days.
- Cutoff timezone: Eastern Standard Time (New York).

Merchant Center changes may take 24-72 hours to clear from the `Needs attention` tab.
