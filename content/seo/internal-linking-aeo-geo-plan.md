# Internal Linking, AEO, GEO, And AI-Search Plan

## Internal Linking Rules

- Homepage should link to Best Sellers, Name Necklaces, Initial Necklaces, Birthstone Jewelry, Gifts, Mother's Collection, Wedding & Bridesmaids, Couple Jewelry, and Jewelry Care.
- Primary navigation should cover high-intent collection categories, not every possible sub-intent.
- Featured navigation can reinforce priority collections across templates.
- Each collection should link to 3-6 related collections and one relevant guide.
- Each product page should link to its primary collection, Jewelry Care, FAQ, Shipping, and Returns where UX allows.
- Each blog/guide should link to relevant collections in the first third of the article, contextual body sections, and final next-step section.
- Footer should include FAQ, Contact, Shipping, Returns, Jewelry Care, Personalized Jewelry Guide, AI Brand Information, and policy pages.

## GEO And AI-Search Content Rules

- Put concise answers near the top of pages before long editorial sections.
- Use stable entity language: `North & Pearl`, `personalized jewelry`, `meaningful gifting`, `name necklaces`, `initial jewelry`, `birthstone-inspired jewelry`.
- Keep the AI Brand Information page factual and non-promotional.
- Include claim boundaries directly in AI-facing content.
- Link from AI Brand Information to About, FAQ, primary collections, Jewelry Care, Shipping, Returns, and Contact.
- Do not add unsupported sameAs profiles, awards, reviews, certifications, or supplier/manufacturing claims.

## Organic Pinterest Strategy

Board taxonomy:

- Personalized Jewelry Gifts
- Name Necklace Ideas
- Initial Jewelry Gifts
- Birthstone-Inspired Jewelry
- Jewelry Gifts for Mom
- Bridesmaid Jewelry Gifts
- Anniversary Jewelry Gifts
- Jewelry Care Tips

Pin standards:

- Title should match one clear intent, e.g. `Personalized Name Necklace Gift Idea`.
- Description should mention recipient/occasion and link to the relevant collection or guide.
- Avoid material/performance claims unless verified.
- Use product-inspectable images, clean backgrounds, and vertical crops where possible.

## Organic Shopping Readiness

Pre-feed organic checklist:

- Clear product title.
- Accurate price and availability.
- Product-specific description without unsupported claims.
- High-quality product image.
- Correct product category/type.
- Shipping and returns policies complete.
- Variant data clean.
- Product identifiers handled correctly.
- No mismatch between page content, schema, and feed fields.

## Measurement Plan

Track organic progress through:

- Google Search Console index coverage.
- Search Console queries by collection and guide.
- Indexed product count versus intended indexable product count.
- Organic landing pages by session and conversion path.
- Internal search queries from Shopify.
- Product page add-to-cart rate from organic landing pages.
- Blog-to-collection click-through.
- Pinterest outbound clicks and saves from organic pins.

Do not invent baseline values. Establish baselines only after data access is available.
