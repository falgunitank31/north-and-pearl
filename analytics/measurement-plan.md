# Measurement Plan

## Goals

- Measure ecommerce conversion rate.
- Track product discovery paths.
- Track personalization intent.
- Track cart and checkout funnel behavior.
- Track email signup performance.

## Tools To Evaluate

- Shopify analytics
- GA4
- Microsoft Clarity
- Shopify Search & Discovery reporting

## Order-Readiness Measurement Checklist

Required before reporting traffic/order improvement:

- Confirm GA4 property exists and is connected to the Shopify storefront.
- Confirm Google Search Console is verified for `https://northandpearl.com`.
- Submit or confirm Shopify sitemap in Search Console.
- Confirm Bing Webmaster Tools verification.
- Confirm Shopify analytics can report sessions, product views, add-to-cart, checkout starts, purchases, and revenue.
- Confirm Microsoft Clarity is either intentionally not installed or installed only after privacy review.
- Confirm Shopify Customer Privacy settings match the tracking plan.

## Launch KPI Baseline

Do not invent baseline metrics. Once data sources are verified, record:

- Organic sessions.
- Direct sessions.
- Referral sessions.
- Top organic landing pages.
- Product views.
- Collection views.
- Add-to-cart rate.
- Checkout start rate.
- Conversion rate.
- Orders.
- Revenue.
- Email signups.
- Search Console impressions.
- Search Console clicks.
- Indexed page count.

## Weekly Reporting Cadence

- Monday: verify previous-week traffic, landing pages, and conversion funnel.
- Friday: summarize changes, blockers, and agent handoffs for order readiness.

## Agent Handoffs

- Faraday receives verified organic traffic, query, and landing-page data.
- Kuhn receives UX signals such as high-traffic/low-click or high-view/low-add-to-cart pages.
- Gauss receives product/category performance and catalog gaps.
- Tesla receives tracking, rendering, speed, schema, or cart issues.
- Wegener receives support, shipping, returns, and fulfillment-related customer friction signals.

## Consent And Privacy

No analytics implementation should occur until tracking, consent, and privacy implications are reviewed.
