# Product SEO Standards

## Product Title Standard

Use:
- Brand + product descriptor only when needed for clarity.
- Distinct names that avoid duplicates.
- Product type in the title.

Avoid:
- Generic duplicate titles like `North & Pearl Heart Necklace` repeated many times.
- Unsupported material claims.
- Supplier listing language copied from Alibaba.
- Keyword stuffing.

Recommended pattern:
- `North & Pearl [Design/Shape/Meaning] [Product Type]`
- Example: `North & Pearl Initial Shell Necklace`

## Product Description Structure

1. Emotional short description: 1-2 original sentences.
2. Why it is meaningful: gifting, milestone, self-expression.
3. Product details: only confirmed facts.
4. Personalization instructions: only when supported by product/options.
5. Gift note: gift-ready positioning without unsupported packaging promises unless confirmed.
6. Care guidance: general gentle-care language.
7. FAQs: visible on page if FAQ schema is ever used.

## Claim Safety

Use `UNKNOWN` internally when materials, plating, stone, testing reports, or origin are not confirmed.

Do not publish:
- hypoallergenic
- waterproof
- tarnish-free
- nickel-free
- lead-free
- cadmium-free
- sterling silver
- gold vermeil
- solid gold
- handmade
- ethically sourced
- sustainable

unless supplier documentation and owner approval exist.

## Metadata Standard

SEO title:
- Product name + `| North & Pearl`
- Keep concise.

Meta description:
- Mention product type, gift angle, and personalization or occasion if true.
- No unsupported material, quality, shipping, or warranty claims.

## Image SEO Standard

Alt text should describe the visible product:
- `gold-tone initial shell necklace with letter charm`
- `heart pendant necklace on neutral product background`

Avoid:
- Keyword stuffing.
- Claims not visible in the image.
- Saying the exact material unless confirmed.

## Organic Shopping Readiness

Every active product should have:
- Accurate price.
- Accurate availability.
- At least one clear product image.
- A unique title.
- A description that explains what the customer receives.
- Product type/category.
- No invented GTIN, MPN, certification, or material data.

## Current High-Risk Cleanup Queue

INFERENCE from recent product QA output:
- Duplicate or generic product names need cleanup before scaling to 100 products.
- Products with only one image should be prioritized for image expansion.
- Product descriptions imported from source data need review for originality and claim safety.
