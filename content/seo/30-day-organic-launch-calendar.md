## Publishing Gates

- Owner/orchestrator approval.
- No unsupported material, stone, allergy, durability, shipping, review, or sales claims.
- Collection has active products.
- Internal links resolve.
- Mobile layout reviewed.
- FAQ schema, if added, exactly matches visible FAQ content.

## Reusable Daily Organic Checklist

- One commercial page improved or one guide advanced.
- At least 3 internal links added or mapped.
- At least 2 organic Pinterest pins drafted.
- Claim safety reviewed.
- No paid channel action taken.
