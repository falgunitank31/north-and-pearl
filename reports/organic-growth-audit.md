## Key Problems

Critical:
- Duplicate and generic product names can confuse shoppers, search engines, and AI systems.
- Product facts/material claims remain unconfirmed and must stay conservative.

High:
- Collection pages need unique intent, metadata, intro copy, bottom SEO copy, and internal links.
- Product descriptions need uniqueness and claim-safety review before catalog scales further.
- Products with only one image are weaker for conversion and image search.

Medium:
- AEO question map needs publishing into visible FAQ/guide content.
- Pinterest organic and free Shopping readiness need product data discipline.
- Blog/content roadmap should support commercial pages, not outrun them.
