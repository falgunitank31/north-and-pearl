# Mobile UX Standard

Mobile is the primary storefront.

## Required Checks

- Header, menu, search, hero, product grids, filters, sorting, product forms, and cart drawer work on mobile.
- Tap targets are large enough.
- Product cards remain readable without becoming oversized.
- No horizontal scrolling.
- Product image crops show the jewelry clearly.
- Sticky or prominent add-to-cart behavior does not hide content.
- Personalization fields are easy to complete.
- Section spacing supports shopping speed.
