# Product And Collection Foundation

## Branch

`agent/website-architect-product-collection-foundation`

## Purpose

Establish the first Shopify collection and product page foundations for North & Pearl using native Dawn Online Store 2.0 templates and blocks.

## Scope

### Collection Template

- Enables collection image display.
- Uses ivory/taupe brand color scheme.
- Sets product grid to 24 products per page.
- Enables filtering and sorting.
- Enables standard quick add.
- Adds an SEO/supporting rich text block below the product grid.

### Product Template

- Adds emotional product note.
- Adds trust icon row.
- Keeps variant picker, quantity selector, and buy buttons.
- Adds collapsible sections for personalization, materials, gift packaging, shipping/returns, jewelry care, and FAQs.
- Keeps related product recommendations.

### Personalized Product Template

Adds `templates/product.personalized.json` as a future assignable template for custom/name/initial/birthstone products.

## Safety Notes

- No live theme was modified.
- No theme was published.
- No unsupported material claims were added.
- Personalization fields are not fully implemented yet because Shopify product option/metafield/app strategy must be confirmed first.
- Shipping, production, returns, packaging, and material copy remains placeholder or explicitly pending confirmation.

- Confirm material facts with suppliers.

- Create Shopify collections matching homepage links.
- Assign `product.personalized` template to personalized products after catalog creation.
