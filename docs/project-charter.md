# Project Charter

## Brand

North & Pearl

## Domain

northandpearl.com

## Shopify Store

https://north-and-pearl.myshopify.com

## Business

Premium DTC personalized jewelry and meaningful gifting brand.

## Brand Promise

Crafted for life's meaningful moments.

## Primary Objective

Build a premium, original, conversion-focused Shopify Online Store 2.0 website that supports long-term growth across ecommerce UX, SEO, AI search, merchandising, brand trust, and operational readiness.

## Target Customers

- Women ages 22-45
- Gift buyers
- Couples
- Mothers
- Bridesmaids
- Birthday and anniversary shoppers
- Men buying gifts for partners
- Corporate gifting buyers

## Non-Negotiables

- No live theme changes without approval.
- No publishing without separate approval.
- No competitor copying.
- No unsupported material or fulfillment claims.
- No secrets or customer data in the repository.
- All Shopify implementation must be reversible where practical.
