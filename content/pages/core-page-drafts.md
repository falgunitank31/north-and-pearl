## Jewelry Care Guide

Store jewelry separately in a soft pouch or jewelry box. Keep pieces away from harsh chemicals, lotions, perfumes, and extended moisture unless the specific product page confirms otherwise. Clean gently with a soft dry cloth.

## Personalized Jewelry Guide

Choose personalization that feels meaningful and easy to wear. Names, initials, birth-month details, dates, coordinates, and short phrases can all tell a story. For gifts, consider the recipient's everyday style, the occasion, and whether they prefer subtle or statement pieces.
