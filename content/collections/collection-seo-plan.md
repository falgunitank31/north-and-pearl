# Collection SEO Plan

## Priority Collections

- Shop All: Personalized jewelry and meaningful gifts across necklaces, bracelets, rings, earrings, gift boxes, and gift cards.
- Best Sellers: Launch collection for highest-confidence gifting products.
- Name Necklaces: Name-based personalized necklaces for birthdays, anniversaries, mothers, bridesmaids, and self-gifting.
- Initial Necklaces: Minimal personalized initial jewelry for everyday wear and gifting.
- Birthstone Jewelry: Birth-month inspired personalized gifts, pending supplier confirmation for exact stones and materials.
- Couple Jewelry: Personalized jewelry for anniversaries, relationships, coordinates, names, and dates.
- Gifts: Gift-first merchandising by recipient, occasion, and budget.
- Mother's Collection: Personalized family, name, initial, and birthstone-inspired pieces for mothers.
- Wedding & Bridesmaids: Bridal party keepsakes, thank-you gifts, and wedding milestone jewelry.
- Men's Jewelry: Personalized giftable jewelry direction pending supplier confirmation.

## SEO Requirements

- Each collection needs one unique H1, one 120-160 character meta description, and a 150-300 word collection description.
- Internal links should connect gift collections to guides, FAQ, shipping, returns, and care content.
- Avoid material claims until supplier documents confirm them.
- Use descriptive image alt text such as `personalized name necklace gift for her`.

## AI Search Requirements

- Collection copy should answer who the collection is for, what the products are, what occasions they fit, and which claims are confirmed.
- Add FAQ blocks to high-intent collections after final policies and fulfillment timelines are approved.
