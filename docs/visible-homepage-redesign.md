# Visible Homepage Redesign

## Reason

The previous homepage relied too heavily on Dawn-native sections and looked under-designed when real product imagery and Shopify content were not available yet.

## Change

Added a custom `north-pearl-homepage` section with a complete visible premium storefront experience:

- Luxury hero with custom jewelry and packaging placeholders.
- Trust bar.
- Category cards.
- How-it-works split section.
- Gift-by-occasion band.
- Review/value cards.
- Email capture.

## Safety

- No live theme publication.
- No competitor assets.
- No unsupported material claims.
- Placeholder visuals are CSS-based and original.
